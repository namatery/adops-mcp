# 1. Kill wasteful spenders
pause anything spending over $50 with no conversions in the last 48 hours
  -> matches: Holiday Gift Guide - Desktop ($63.40, 0 conv), Cold Prospecting - Broad ($71.50, 0 conv)

# 2. Scale the winners
double the daily budget on any campaign with CPA under $20
  -> matches the low-CPA campaigns (e.g. Retargeting - Warm Cart $7.33, Winter Sale - Mobile $14.00)

# 3. Trim underperformers
drop bids 15% on campaigns with CTR below 0.5%
  -> matches low-CTR campaigns (e.g. Brand Awareness - Native, ctr ~0.32%)

# 4. Combined example
pause campaigns over $60 spend with zero conversions, and raise budget 50% on campaigns with CPA under $10
